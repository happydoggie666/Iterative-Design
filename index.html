2.1 Interactive High Fedelity Mockup

Original version before studio Critique:

Todo: Take into account the design principles that you've learned in class, and briefly explain your design choices

Summary of studio critique feedback:

Overall Design:
  • Screen change in size
  • It would be better if the bottom tab can show which page is currently on
  • Icon, text and margin/padding should be standardized
Account page:
  • Text is too small
Cart page:
  • It would be better if users can go directly into the cart and back from the cart. A “back” button is needed to supplement the way users can go back from the cart to other pages (the old version's only way is using the bottom tab)
  • Cart should emphasize the total final price. It would be nice if our design can show the dynamic update of the items & the final price.
  • We should add the “+”, “-” button to allows the users to increase / decrease items in cart
  • Nice if there is indication of what items is in the cart
  • Cart's delivery date session is a little bit confusing
  • The bottom's recommendation session's title can lead to some confusion. It would be better if we use larger size title
  • In some pages (i.e. home page), don't know how to open the cart, the right top button is too small
Community:
  • Community's follower feature need some interaction design
Category:
  • Category's icon vs text is confusing
Profile:
  • Profile should link directed to the cart
  • In profile's my order session, there are two button at the bottom “Saved items” & “Buy again”, they are not arranged at the same hierarchy with other buttons

User Test Tasks:

This is an interactive mockup(prototype) of a grocery shopping application - Dropezy, which is designed to deliver orders from groceries in 15 minutes. Notice that as this is not a real application, some parts might not fully function as expected.

Imagine you are a user who wants to buy some food.
Please browse the application as you like, and purchase two boxes of cheese mac.

  1. Please enter the application and purchase two boxes of Annie's Shells & White Cheddar Macaroni & Cheese.
  2. Please find and browse the recipe of “healthy salmon for lunch” and tell us how you like it
  3. Please explore the “following” feature and share your experience and thought with us
  4. Like the item “100% Pure pumpkin” and check the saved item in account page - my list session
